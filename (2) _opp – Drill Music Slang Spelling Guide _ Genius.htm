Firearm Magazines

• magazine | mag | mag'

• KRISS Vector | KRISS Vec' | KRISS | Vec' | Vect' | Kris | kriss vec

• thirty-round mag/clip | fifty-round mag/clip | hundred-round mag/clip | 30-round mag/clip | 50-round mag/clip | 100-round mag/clip

There's a common misconception that "clip" and "magazine" are interchangeable terms, but they're not, it's just the artists not knowing. (still added it anyway)

When an artist raps, "I got my thirty/fifty on me" or something similar, it should be written out — they're almost always referring to magazine size, not a caliber or model.

For annotators: Never use both terms together (like "[?]-round magazine clip"). It should just be "[?]-round magazine. Also, do not put a period before the number (like ".30-round magazine"). It just refers to the number of rounds in the magazine and has nothing to do with calibers.

How to Format Lyrics:

  • Type out all lyrics, even repeating song parts like the chorus
  • Lyrics should be broken down into individual lines
  • Use section headers above different song parts like [Verse], [Chorus], etc.
  • Use italics (<i>lyric</i>) and bold (<b>lyric</b>) to distinguish between different vocalists in the same song part
  • If you don’t understand a lyric, use [?]
